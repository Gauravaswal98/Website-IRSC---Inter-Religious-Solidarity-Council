Our Vision

A harmonious India and world where people of all faiths are free to practice without encroachment, living in shared commitment to peace, justice, and human dignity.

Who We Are

The Inter-Religious Solidarity Council (IRSC) is a network of faith leaders, activists, scholars, and journalists. We provide a space for interfaith engagement that reinforces shared values underpinning all major faith traditions.

Our Mission

Dialogue

Promoting mutual understanding between diverse religions, faiths, and spiritual traditions.

Resistance

Standing up against extremism, absolutism, and supremacist ideas in the name of religion.

Values

Promoting values of love, empathy, solidarity, and inclusiveness while discouraging consumerism.

Action

Collaborating with institutions to serve the needy through socially relevant services.

Bridges

Building bridges across faiths through education, advocacy, and joint action in Mumbai.

Reviving the ethical teachings of coexistence.

The Rationale

While religion is often framed as a source of conflict, we believe it guides the human conscience toward compassion, truth, and nonviolence.

In the present sociopolitical context—marked by increasing extremist expressions—there is an urgent need to amplify the ethical teachings that emphasize coexistence. We aim to revive these inclusive traditions to heal social divisions.
