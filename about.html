Who We Are

The Inter-Religious Solidarity Council (IRSC) is a network of faith leaders, activists, scholars, and journalists. We provide a space for interfaith engagement that reinforces shared values underpinning all major faith traditions.
A harmonious India and world where people of all faiths are free to practice without encroachment, living in shared commitment to peace, justice, and human dignity.
